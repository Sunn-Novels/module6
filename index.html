Biography of Robyn Copeland

Robyn Copeland was born on June 13th, 1988 in Muncie, Indiana. She has three pet cats named Snowy, Princess, and Panther (Princess and Snowy pictured on the right.) They are all very large cats, but super cute and friendly. She also collects octopi plushies.

She has a dream of being a well known author one day, and enjoys writing young adult fantasy stories.

Her favorite color is pink. And also rainbow.

She also loves to sing, write music, and poems.

Currently


Robyn works for a public library in circulation, but would love to be in cataloging or part of an academic library.
A career in writing would be the ultimate goal though.

Future

Robyn is about halfway through her MLIS program, currently with a focus on Academic Libraries, but is still unsure of the future.
She is really enjoying learning coding and almost wishes she got a Bachelor's degree in computer science rather than physics. Almost.
