Biography of Robyn Copeland

Robyn Copeland was born on June 13th, 1988 in Muncie, Indiana. She has three pet cats named Snowy, Princess, and Panther (Snowy and Princess pictured on the right.) They are all very large cats, but super cute and friendly. She also collects octopi plushies.

She has a dream of being a well known author one day, and enjoys writing young adult fantasy stories.

Her favorite color is pink. And also rainbow.

She also loves to sing, write music, and poems.

Currently, Robyn works for a public library in circulation, but would love to be in cataloging or part of an academic library. A career in writing would be the ultimate goal though.
